Welcome to Hotel

Amora , Rajnandgaon

Hotel Amora is nestled in Rajnandgaon, focuses on Peace and Harmony that thrives amongst people of Various Regions of this place, bounded by ponds and rivers, and is famed for some small-scale industries and trades. The hotel offers various amenities which include a front desk and plenty of others.

It is an ideal place for meeting up your folks for a get-today, celebrating a birthday, anniversary, farewell party for your loved ones. For accommodation, the hotel features abundant and spotlessly clean rooms equipped with various conveniences such as attached bathrooms, hot and cold water. The rooms here are perfectly designed and crafted as per the taste of guests to make their stay relaxed and comfortable.
